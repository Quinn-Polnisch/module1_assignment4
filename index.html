About

Coding Temple Park is a brand new park in the heart of downtown Chicago. The park offers a wide variety of areas for all ages. One of the most popular area is pictured to the left, our chess area. We also have a wide variety of fountains and play structures. We even included a large selection of walking and running paths for our visitors to enjoy. To see more photos go to our Gallery.
